Editor's Note

In today's digital world, technology connects people faster than ever before. While the internet offers endless opportunities for learning, communication, and creativity, it also requires responsibility. Digital citizenship reminds us that our actions online have real-world consequences.
By practicing respect, responsibility, and awareness online, we can help create safer and more positive digital spaces. Understanding digital citizenship empowers individuals to use technology wisely while protecting themselves and others.
